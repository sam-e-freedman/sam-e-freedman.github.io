Featured Thesis

Post-fire carbon dynamics in California forests

My M.S. thesis in Geographic Information Science investigates post-fire ecosystem recovery and carbon dynamics across the CZU Lightning Complex and Dixie Fire. The project integrates airborne LiDAR, GEDI, satellite imagery, burn severity data, vegetation layers, and machine learning models to understand how forest carbon changes after wildfire.
